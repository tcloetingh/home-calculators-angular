  • Not sure about KwH? The national average is $0.13
  • Heating Degree Days (HDD)? take the average temperature on any given day and subtract it from the base temperature (65 degrees). Add up all the days throughout the cold season to get Heating Degree Days. weatherdatadepot.com/degree-day-comparison is a great site to find annual HDD by zipcode.
  • Cooling Degree Days (CDD)? Just the inverse of HDD. The aggregated total of average temperature per day above 65 degrees. If unsure use 3500 for both HDD and CDD, thats about the average for the mid-atlantic region.
